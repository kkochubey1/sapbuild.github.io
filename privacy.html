Use of Google Analytics

The Website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses “cookies,” which are text files placed on your computer to help the website analyze how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google’s behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google.

SAP may use this information to evaluate your use of the Website and to make improvements to the Website or Service.

You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. Additionally, you may use the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on developed by Google and available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en. The Add-on communicates with the Google Analytics JavaScript (ga.js) to indicate that information about the website visit should not be sent to Google Analytics.

Use of Piwik

Some parts of this Website use an analytics tool provided by Piwik. The Piwik tool collects your IP address and stores a truncated version of it. By default, all website visitors to that portion of the Website using Piwik are tracked, but you may opt-out by going to this URL: https://piwik.itc.sap.com/piwik/index.php?module=CoreAdminHome&action=optOut&language=en.

This will set a cookie called ‘piwik_ignore’. Visitors with a piwik_ignore cookie will not be tracked. If you refresh your cookies, you may need to opt out again.

Cookies/Do Not Track
